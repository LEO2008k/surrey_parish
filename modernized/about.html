Welcome to Our Parish

Our Mission & Community

Our parish opens its doors and heart to the Catholic community in the Surrey area. Come celebrate the Divine Liturgy with us on Sunday and afterwards join our parish community as we enjoy coffee, tea, treats and fellowship.

The Exaltation of the Holy Cross Ukrainian Catholic Parish is a vibrant community rooted in faith, tradition, and service. We strive to provide a spiritual home for all those seeking a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ through the ancient and beautiful traditions of the Byzantine Rite.

Church Image

HC Volunteers (UCWLC)

HC Volunteers strengthen and supports our parish through volunteer activities. We make and sell traditional, homemade dishes and prepare meals and snacks for parish events.

Parish History

Founded by dedicated Ukrainian immigrants, our church has stood as a beacon of faith in Surrey for decades, preserving cultural heritage and spiritual guidance.
